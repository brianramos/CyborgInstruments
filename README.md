**Stackable Modes:**
- Click multiple beat buttons to layer rhythms together (they toggle on/off)
- Click multiple melody buttons to stack harmonic voices
- Active modes show as tags at the bottom of the touch pad
- Automated beat patterns play continuously while you touch the pad

**XY Touch Control:**
- **Y-axis (vertical)**: Controls pitch - drag up for higher notes, down for lower
- **X-axis (horizontal)**: Controls tone/filter - left is warm/soft, right is bright/crystal
- Real-time continuous control as you drag your finger
- Multi-touch creates up to 5 simultaneous voices

**Per-Scene Flavor:**
- **Rainbow Meadow**: Warm, sparkly tones with soft sine/triangle waves
- **Crystal Cave**: Edgier synths with sawtooth/square waves and resonance
- **Cloud Kingdom**: Dreamy, airy textures with gentle filtering

**How to Play:**
1. Tap beat & melody buttons to activate layers (they stay on until toggled off)
2. Touch and drag on the canvas to play - position controls the sound
3. Use multiple fingers for rich harmonies
4. Swipe left/right to travel between the three fluff kingdoms!
